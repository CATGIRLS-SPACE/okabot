# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 27, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how **CATGIRLS SPACE** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use **okabot**, its accompanying website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

We believe privacy is extremely important and are committed to collecting only the information necessary to provide our Services.

By using okabot or its website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account and Discord Information

To provide our Services, we may collect and store information including:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server (Guild) IDs
* Language and localization preferences
* Server configuration and preferences
* User preferences and settings
* User profile data including but not limited to:

  * XP
  * Inventory
  * Customizations
  * Achievements

This information is used solely to operate bot features and remember your settings.

### Website Authentication

Our website allows users to sign in using Discord in order to manage their servers without using Discord commands.

Authentication is performed through Discord. We only receive the information necessary to identify your Discord account and determine which servers you are authorized to manage.

### Logs and Diagnostics

To maintain, improve, and secure our Services, we may collect diagnostic information including:

* Command usage
* Error reports
* Debug logs
* Moderation logs
* Anonymous or aggregated analytics

These logs help us diagnose issues, improve features, detect abuse, and maintain service reliability.

### Optional Features

Certain optional features may collect additional information, such as Discord usernames or display names.

These features are only enabled in servers that are 
    a. explicitly allowed by CATGIRLS SPACE administrators,
    b. explicitly choose to use them, and 
    c. where all users have provided any required consent.

We employ reasonable administrative and technical measures designed to protect stored information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While no system can guarantee absolute security, we will continuously protect your information to the best of our abilities.
